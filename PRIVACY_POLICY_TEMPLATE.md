## YouTube Integration

When you connect your YouTube account to Uplora, we request access to upload videos to your YouTube channel.

### What We Access
- **Video Uploads**: We can upload videos to your YouTube channel only when you explicitly request an upload through our Upload interface
- **Channel Information**: Basic channel details (name, ID) to display connection status

### What We DON'T Access
- Your existing YouTube videos, playlists, or channel content
- Your email address, contacts, or other Google account information
- Any data beyond what's necessary for video uploads
- Your YouTube analytics or private data

### How We Store Your Data
- **OAuth Tokens**: We store encrypted OAuth refresh tokens on our secure servers
- **Token Usage**: We only use tokens to upload videos when you explicitly request it
- **Token Renewal**: We automatically refresh tokens to maintain upload functionality
- **Data Retention**: Tokens are stored until you disconnect your YouTube account

### Your Control
- **Disconnect Anytime**: You can disconnect your YouTube account from Settings → Social
- **Token Deletion**: When you disconnect, we immediately delete your OAuth tokens
- **No Automatic Uploads**: We never upload videos without your explicit request

### Data Sharing
- **No Third-Party Sharing**: We do not share your Google/YouTube data with third parties
- **Service Providers**: We only use the data to provide the upload functionality you requested
- **Legal Requirements**: We may share data if required by law or to protect our rights

### Security
- **Encryption**: All OAuth tokens are encrypted at rest
- **Secure Transmission**: All data is transmitted over HTTPS
- **Access Control**: Only authorized personnel can access token data for support purposes

### Compliance
We comply with:
- Google API Services User Data Policy (Limited Use)
- YouTube API Services Terms & Policies
- Applicable data protection laws
